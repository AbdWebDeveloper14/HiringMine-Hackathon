HiringMine

Rooted in the belief that every individual holds untapped brilliance, HiringMine is a talent discovery and hiring platform where raw potential meets real opportunity.

Founded by Jaffar Aman & Muhammad Sufiyan , HiringMine is a launchpad for students and freshers to learn, grow, and get discovered. Our mission? To equip emerging talent with the tools and exposure they need to shine bright — right from the mine.
